## Score Interpretation

| Range | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| **90-100%** | Excellent — content meets standards with minor or no issues | Ready for publication |
| **70-89%** | Good — content is effective but has areas for improvement | Address identified issues before publication |
| **50-69%** | Needs Work — content has significant gaps in usability or voice | Revise and re-score before proceeding |
| **Below 50%** | Major Revision — content does not meet minimum standards | Return to drafting (STEP-005) with specific feedback |

## Editing Phase Checklist

After scoring, apply the four editing phases from *Strategic Writing for UX*:

- [ ] **Purposeful**: Does every piece of text meet its purpose for both the person and the organization?
- [ ] **Concise**: Is the text as short as possible while retaining meaning?
- [ ] **Conversational**: Would the text feel natural if spoken aloud? Is it recognizable as the product's voice?
- [ ] **Clear**: Will the reader understand immediately, without having to think?
