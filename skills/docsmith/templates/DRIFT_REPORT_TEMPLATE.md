## Confidence rubric

| Confidence | Meaning                                                                       | Default decision |
| ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| HIGH       | Pure label / text change, location unchanged, no semantic shift               | `auto-fix`       |
| MEDIUM     | Structural change but unambiguous (field added, option renamed)               | `prompt user`    |
| LOW        | Major flow change, element missing, or AI uncertain how to fix                | `skip`           |

AI must err toward LOW when uncertain. False HIGH (auto-applied wrong fix) is far worse than false LOW (forced manual review).
